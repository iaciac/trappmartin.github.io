I'm a PhD student working on Sum-Product Networks and Bayesian nonparametrics with applications to cognitive sciences and natural language processing. My association is with the Austrian Research Institute for Artificial Intelligence (OFAI) in Vienna and the Graz University of Technology. I'm supervised by the head of the OFAI Prof. em. Robert Trappl and by Assoc. Prof. Franz Pernkopf from the Laboratory of Signal Processing and Speech Communication, Graz University of Technology.
Previously, I have been working as a researcher in medical and biological image processing at the VRVis Research Center for Virtual Reality and Visualisation and have conducted my Masters thesis at the Vienna University of Technology on Fast and Accurate Automatic Localization of Anatomical Landmarks on Medical Images.
